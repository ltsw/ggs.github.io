NATIONAL LEARN TO SWIM WEEK

March 29th to April 2nd 2016


What is Learn to Swim Week?

Grenada is an island country in the Caribbean about 100 miles north of Venezuela. Like other Caribbean countries many National Grenadians, children and adults cannot swim (estimated at over 90%) resulting in unfortunate drownings.

Spearheaded by Deb Eastwood and the Grenada Youth Adventurers, National Learn to Swim Week (NLTSW) is an initiative to provide free swim lessons to children and adults throughout Grenada. The pilot program launched over the Easter of 2015 welcomed over 500 new swimmers (100 of them adults) at 11 different beaches and pools.

NLTSW 2015 was an inprecedented success with over 100 volunteers and the support of sponsors such as Republic Bank Ltd, The Jason Roberts Foundation and The Grenada Olympic Association. Endorsed by the Government of Grenada Ministry of Sport and the Grenada Amateur Swimming Assoicaiton, NLTSW looks to become an integral part of the local sporting scene.
